GAME RULES:
1. Draw the board. First, we have to draw the board, which is made up of a 3x3 grid of squares.
2. Have the first player go first. The first player goes with "X".
3. Have the second player go second. The first player goes with "0".
4. Keep alternating moves until one of the players has drawn a row of three symbols or until
no one can win.
